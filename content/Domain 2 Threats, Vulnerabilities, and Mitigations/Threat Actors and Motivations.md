Threat Actors
- Nation state: national interests, sophisticated attacks
- Advanced Persistent Thread (APT): cause damage over an extended period
- Unskilled attacker: script kiddies, tools from dark web
- Hacktivists: ideological, political, or social motives
- Insider threats: from within an organization, may be unintentional
- Organized crime: monetary gains
- Shadow IT: technology within an organization used without approval, may not be malicious

Threat Actors 
- Internal / External
- Well-resourced / Limited resources
- Highly sophisticated / Less sophisticated

Motivations:
- Data Exfiltration
- Espionage
- Service Disruption
- Blackmail
- Financial Gain
- Philosophical / Political Beliefs
- Ethics
- Revenge
- Disruption / Chaos
- War

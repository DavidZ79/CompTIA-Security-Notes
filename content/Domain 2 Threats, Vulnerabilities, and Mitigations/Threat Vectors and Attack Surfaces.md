Vulnerable Software

Vulerable Software Scanning:
- Client-based scanning: operates on client device
- Agentless scanning: scan without installations (Nmap, Wireshark)

Unsecure Networks
- Wireless networks: unsecure. Disable Service Set Identifier (SSID) broadcast, Mac filtering
- Wired networks
- Bluetooth: Personal Area Network (PAN)

Open Service Ports: Can result in unauthorized access and the execution of malicious code

Default Credentials

Supply Chain
- Managed Service Providers (MSPs): third party that fulfills all a company's IT needs
- Vendors
- Suppliers

Human Vectors / Social Engineering
- Phishing: Untargeted deception to lure victims for their personal information
- Spear Phishing: Targeted phishing for higher-ups
- Smishing: SMS phishing
- Misinformation / Disinformation: manipulate perceptions and decisions
- Impersonation: adopt a false identity
- Business Email Compromise: Use compromised email for financial fraud
- Pretexting: fabricating a scenario to get information
- Watering Hole Attacks: compromise legitimate websites
- Brand Impersonation
- Typosquatting: Registered domains with subtle differences to the real domain

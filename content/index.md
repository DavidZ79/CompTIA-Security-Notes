## Content Overview

### [Domain 1: General Security Concepts](./Domain%201%20General%20Security%20Concepts/)
Fundamental security concepts, security controls, change management processes, and cryptographic solutions.

### [Domain 2: Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations](./Domain%202%20Threats%20Vulnerabilities%20and%20Mitigations/)
Understanding threats, identifying vulnerabilities, and implementing mitigation strategies.

### [Domain 3: Security Architecture](./Domain%203%20Security%20Architecture/)
Security architecture models, secure enterprise infrastructure, data protection strategies, and resilience planning.

### [Domain 4: Security Operations](./Domain%204%20Security%20Operations/)
Identity management, security monitoring, incident response, vulnerability management, and security automation.

### [Domain 5: Security Program Management and Oversight](./Domain%205%20Security%20Program%20Management/)
Security governance, risk management, compliance, security awareness, and third-party risk management.

## Recommended Resources

### [Exam Objectives 🔗](https://assets.ctfassets.net/82ripq7fjls2/6TYWUym0Nudqa8nGEnegjG/0f9b974d3b1837fe85ab8e6553f4d623/CompTIA-Security-Plus-SY0-701-Exam-Objectives.pdf)
CompTIA's official syllabus for potential exam content for the Security+ SY0-701 exam. The most valuable study resource, which is also free. It also contains a comprehensive list of technical acronyms which may show up on the exam.

### [Professor Messer 🔗](https://www.professormesser.com/security-plus/sy0-701/sy0-701-video/sy0-701-comptia-security-plus-course/)

Professor Messer has a complete set of great videos specifically for SY0-701. 
They are relatively short and concise, and are great for conveying the ideas of any topic. 
He also occasionally hosts [live exam review sessions 🔗](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wwr8dobMLiQ) with previous exam problems on his YouTube channel.

### [Ian Neil's SY0-701 Certification Guide 🔗](https://www.amazon.ca/dp/1835461530?ref=ppx_yo2ov_dt_b_fed_asin_title)
*CompTIA Security+ SY0-701 Certification Guide - Third Edition* is my preferred textbook, and is my primary resource in studying for this exam. 

Caution of unreputable textbook authors on Amazon, as they often mostly contain shallow or AI generated content. 
They are poorly made with Amazon's digital publishing service so they can slap on a price tag.

### [IT Certification Roadmap](https://partners.comptia.org/docs/default-source/resources/it-certification-roadmap-8-5x11-print0c3c382c09d76fc19da8ff0a002c2329)
A little bit outdated, but gives a general understanding of certification progression/difficulty.

> [!quote] Definition
> **Security Implications** are the security considerations and requirements that must be addressed throughout the entire lifecycle of organizational assets, from acquisition to disposal.

## Acquisition and Procurement

Security considerations during the asset acquisition process:

<details>
<summary><strong>Procurement Security Process</strong></summary>

*Security requirements for acquiring new assets*

**Key Considerations:**
- **Change Management** - Managing security changes during acquisition
- **Vendor Selection** - Evaluating vendor security practices
- **Total Cost of Ownership** - Including security costs in TCO calculations
- **Risk Assessment** - Evaluating security risks of new assets
- **Compliance Alignment** - Ensuring assets meet regulatory requirements

</details>

## Asset Assignment and Accounting

Managing and tracking organizational assets:

<details>
<summary><strong>Asset Management</strong></summary>

*Systematic approach to asset tracking and management*

**Management Components:**
- **Asset Register** - Centralized database of organizational assets
- **Standard Naming Convention** - Consistent asset identification
- **Ownership** - Clear assignment of asset responsibility
- **Classification** - Categorizing assets by sensitivity and value

</details>

## Asset Monitoring and Tracking

Continuous monitoring of organizational assets:

<details>
<summary><strong>Monitoring Strategies</strong></summary>

*Methods for tracking and monitoring assets*

**Monitoring Methods:**
- **Inventory Management** - Regular asset inventory and tracking
- **Enumeration** - Systematic identification and cataloging of assets

</details>

## Asset Disposal and Decommissioning

Secure disposal of assets at end of lifecycle:

<details>
<summary><strong>Secure Disposal Methods</strong></summary>

*Methods for securely disposing of assets and data*

**Disposal Techniques:**
- **Sanitization** - Complete removal of sensitive data
- **Data Wiping** - Overwriting data with random patterns
- **Secure Erase** - Hardware-based data deletion
- **Degaussing** - Magnetic field destruction of data
- **Physical Destruction** - Complete destruction of storage media

**Disposal Process:**
- **Certification** - Documenting secure disposal completion
- **Data Retention** - Compliance with data retention requirements

</details>

> [!note] Vocab Bank
> - **TCO** - Total Cost of Ownership, complete cost of asset ownership
> - **Asset Register** - Centralized database of organizational assets
> - **Sanitization** - Complete removal of sensitive data from storage media
> - **Degaussing** - Using magnetic fields to destroy data on magnetic media
> - **Secure Erase** - Hardware-based method for permanently deleting data
> - **Data Retention** - Legal requirements for keeping data for specified periods
> - **Asset Classification** - Categorizing assets by sensitivity and value
> - **Vendor Selection** - Process of choosing suppliers based on security criteria

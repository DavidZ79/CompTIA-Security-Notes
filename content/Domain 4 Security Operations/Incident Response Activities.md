## Incident Response Process

Systematic approach to handling security incidents:

<details>
<summary><strong>Incident Response Lifecycle</strong></summary>

*Structured process for managing security incidents*

**Response Phases:**
- **Preparation** - Preparing for potential incidents
- **Detection** - Identifying security incidents
- **Analysis** - Analyzing incident details and impact
- **Containment** - Limiting incident spread and damage
- **Eradication** - Removing threats and vulnerabilities
- **Recovery** - Restoring normal operations
- **Lessons Learned** - Improving future response capabilities

</details>

## Threat Analysis Frameworks

Structured approaches to understanding and analyzing threats:

<details>
<summary><strong>MITRE ATT&CK Framework</strong></summary>

*Comprehensive knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques*

**Framework Components:**
- **Adversarial Tactics** - High-level adversary goals
- **Techniques** - Specific methods used by adversaries
- **Common Knowledge** - Shared understanding of threats

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Threat Analysis Models</strong></summary>

*Models for analyzing and understanding security threats*

**Analysis Models:**
- **Cyber Kill Chain** - Stages of cyber attacks
- **Diamond Model** - Four elements of intrusion analysis:
  - **Adversary** - Threat actor characteristics
  - **Capabilities** - Adversary tools and techniques
  - **Infrastructure** - Technical infrastructure used
  - **Victim** - Target organization and assets

</details>
